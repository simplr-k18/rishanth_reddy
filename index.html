About


I like turning messy data and complex problems into things people can actually use. I’ve spent years wiring systems together, building models, and shipping dashboards.. but what I care about most is how the work helps a person make a better call five minutes faster.

I think like a product person: I sketch simple flows, test small ideas, and ask real users what they need. I’m not one of those people who only loves tech — I care about usefulness, clarity, and the quiet satisfaction when something finally clicks.
