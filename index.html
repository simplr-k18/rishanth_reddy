About

I believe great engineering starts with a simple question: What decision is this person trying to make?

Over the past 5 years, I've built data systems processing billions of records across banking and global procurement. But the work I'm most proud of isn't the scale-it's the clarity. The 100+ hours per week saved because teams no longer hunt for data. The 80% improvement in data quality because we changed how we thought about the problem, not just how we coded the solution.

At Baxter International, I built the company's first unified data product by starting with the fundamental problem: procurement teams across 4 continents couldn't trust their own data. I didn't just integrate 10+ systems-I interviewed stakeholders, understood their workflows, and designed a product that 200+ people now rely on daily. At First Citizens Bank, I redesigned core banking architecture by asking: what if we organized data around how people use it, not how systems store it? The result: Customer 360 and 5 other data products that became the foundation for enterprise decision-making.

My approach combines three principles:

1. Start with the problem, not the technology - I interview users, map workflows, understand pain points before writing code. Sometimes the best solution isn't more data-it's better clarity.

2. Build for humans, then optimize for systems - I taught myself Figma to design better interfaces. I create custom dashboards that follow design systems. I believe tools should feel intuitive, not intimidating.

3. Measure what matters - Not lines of code or query performance alone, but: Did this save time? Did this improve decisions? Do people actually want to use this?

This philosophy has led me to create procurement taxonomies (interviewing 30+ stakeholders), build custom internal tools (HTML/CSS/JS, self-taught), and design data products that teams across finance, operations, and C-suite rely on. I work at the intersection of engineering rigor and human-centered design.

>> Currently exploring: How AI can help procurement teams make better sourcing decisions faster-not by replacing human judgment, but by augmenting it with better information at the right moment.

• Loans, FX, Credit Card & Credit Line Data Products - End-to-end analytics platforms for risk, treasury, and consumer credit teams.

Cross-Functional Leadership: Part of Architecture and Center of Excellence teams. Improved data quality across 4 business units working with Agile and SAFe teams. Led governance initiatives for metadata, lineage, and reference data.

Scale: Worked with billions of rows across multiple banking products, maintaining 99.9%+ data quality SLAs for critical financial reporting.
